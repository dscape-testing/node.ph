Node.Philly 2012 is an exclusive evening event for anyone interested in Node.js. In addition to hearing from some of the most influential Node experts, there will be plenty of food and drink, hands-on demos and tons of chances to meet and collaborate with other members of the community.
April 23rd 2012 | University City Science Center

Register

The University Science Center  |  Philadelphia, PA

Schedule

  • 6:45 – 7:30

    Join Paolo Fragomeni for an introduction to the event-driven I/O framework that is changing that way we think about developing web applications.

  • 7:45 – 8:30

    Fully loaded Node! Lloyd Hilaiel will explain how to do a bunch of computation with Node.js, use all available CPUs, fail gracefully, and stay responsive.

  • 8:45 – 9:30

    Charlie Robbins will take us through real-world deployments in business-critical systems and why some of the world's leading companies are choosing Node.

  • 9:45 – 10:30

    James Halliday and Daniel Shaw will show how to use Node.js to enable the real-time streaming web. Guaranteed to generate ideas for next-generation web applications.
