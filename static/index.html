Node.Philly 2012 is an exclusive evening event for anyone interested in Node.js. In addition to hearing from some of the most influential Node experts, there will be plenty of food and drink, hands-on demos and tons of chances to meet and collaborate with other members of the community.
April 23rd 2012 | University Science Center

Register

The University Science Center  |  Philadelphia, PA

Schedule

  • 6:30 – 7:00

    Networking and welcome. Kick off the night with a drink, talk Node, learn about our sponsors, and meet other interesting folks like yourself!

  • 7:00 – 8:00

    Join Mikeal Rogers and Paolo Fragomeni for an introduction to the event-driven I/O framework that is changing that way we think about developing web applications.

  • 8:00 – 9:00

    Charlie Robbins and TBD will take us through real-world deployments in business-critical systems and why some of the world's leading companies are choosing Node.

  • 9:00 – 10:00

    Follow James Halliday and TBD to learn how to use Node.js to enable the real-time streaming web. Guaranteed to generate ideas for next-generation web applications.

Speakers

  • Charlie Robbins  |  CEO, Nodejitsu
    Nodejitsu.com »

    Veteran of the enterprise software world, Charlie previously worked for Microsoft and consulted at several large financial institutions. After discovering Javascript and node.js, he left Wall Street to co-found Nodejitsu in 2010. He spends his free time making hot sauce, coding, contributing, and evangelizing open-source software in New York City. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering from McGill University and is a Masters Candidate at Columbia University.

  • Paolo Fragomeni  |  Co-founder and CTO, Nodejitsu
    Nodejitsu.com »

    Full stack developer, co-founder and CTO of @Nodejitsu, Open Source/Open Web advocate.

  • Mikeal Rogers  |  CEO, Pouch
    Mikeal Rogers.com »

    Mikeal is CEO and Co-Founder at Pouch and Curator of NodeConf. A common name in the node.js community Mikeal held prior positions at Yammer, CouchBase and Mozilla.

  • James Halliday  |  Founder, Browserling
    Browserling.com » Testling.com »
